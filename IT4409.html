Opening Information

The Web Technologies and e-Services course will be open for enrollment starting from April 2025. This course is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of modern web development techniques and e-service applications. It covers a wide range of topics, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, web frameworks, and the architecture behind e-services. The course will be offered in both online and in-person formats, allowing students to choose the most suitable learning method. Enrollment will be open until the end of March 2025.

Seminar Information

A seminar dedicated to Web Technologies and e-Services will be held on April 15, 2025, and is open to all students who are enrolled in the course. During this seminar, industry experts and academic professionals will discuss the latest trends in web development, emerging technologies, and the growing field of e-services. The seminar will also feature case studies of successful e-service implementations, providing valuable insights into real-world applications. Participants will have the opportunity to engage with the speakers and ask questions related to the course material.

Notable News

Several leading tech companies, including some based in the e-commerce, fintech, and cloud computing industries, have shown great interest in the Web Technologies and e-Services course. These companies are eager to find skilled professionals who are proficient in modern web technologies and can contribute to the development of innovative e-service solutions. Many of these companies offer internship and job placement opportunities for top-performing students, and there is strong demand for graduates with expertise in building efficient, scalable, and secure web applications.
